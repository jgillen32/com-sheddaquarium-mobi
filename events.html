Extraordinary Experiences

    • In an unforgettable experience, be face to face with our belugas as you stand side by side with Shedd's animal experts on an underwater ledge. You'll touch the beluga's skin, try a few training techniques and enjoy some surprises.

    • Reservation: Call 312-692-2730

    • Cost: $250 per person (90 minutes)

    • Dates: Friday – Sunday

    • Restrictions: Must be at least 60 inches tall

    • A uniquely engaging beluga encounter for two, complete with a private poolside moment that is perfect for popping the question.

    • Reservation: Call 312-692-2730

    • Cost: $450 for two people

    • Dates: Friday – Sunday

    • Restrictions: Some restrictions apply

  • Penguin Encounter 30 minutes

    • During Shedd's 30-minute penguin encounter, you'll meet to one of our outgoing penguins and even get to touch its sleek, slick feathers.

    • Reservation: Call 312-692-3355

    • Cost: $59.95 (adult) $50.95 (child)

    • Dates: Fridays – Sunday

    • Restrictions: Must be at least 4 years old. 4-11 year olds must be accompanied by a participating paid adult.

    • Pull on a pair of tall rubber boots and go behind the scenes to experience what it's like to be a marine mammal trainer.

    • Reservation: Call 312-692-3224

    • Cost: $350 per person (4 hours)

    • Dates: Fridays and Saturdays

    • Restrictions: Must be at least 12 years old. 12-15 year olds must be accompanied by a participating paid adult.

General Refund and Cancellation Policy

No refunds will be issued unless Shedd Aquarium cancels the program. Shedd Aquarium reserves the right to cancel the event for any reason. If your event is cancelled, you will be informed via phone as soon as possible. In the event of a cancellation, you will be fully refunded within 30 days. Your refund method will be based on your original method of payment.
