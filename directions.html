Directions and Parking

John G. Shedd Aquarium1200 South Lake Shore Drive Chicago, IL 60605

  • Public Transit

    • For all transit information http://www.transitchicago.com/ or call 312-836-7000 Try the CTA Bus Tracker (http://www.ctabustracker.com)

    • The Museum Campus is served year-round by the CTA's #146 bus Service hours are until 10:30 p.m. every day

    • CTA's #130 bus available Memorial Day – Labor Day Service hours are from 9:30 a.m. – 9:30 p.m.

  • Driving Directions

    • From Lake Shore Drive, exit at 18th Street. Follow Museum Campus Drive around Soldier Field. Signs will indicate visitor parking. Shedd is just north of the parking garage and the Field Museum.

  • Parking Options

    • Parking rates start at $19; entry is occasionally on a cash only basis. Rates are higher during special events, and garage access may be restricted to special event ticketholders. For the most current parking information, visit www.soldierfieldparking.com.

    • On most weekends valet service is available to aquarium guests for $25 at Shedd's circular drive (limited availability).
